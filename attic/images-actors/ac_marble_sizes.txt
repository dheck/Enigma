Reference for jump, fall and sink models was gfx32. Other resolutions
adapted s.t. percent-of-tilesize values match best.

Note: To improve the sink/fall illusion, the shadows are shifted towards the marble (to
the top left) while the marble is sinking/falling. We define two conditions that hold:
first:  the shadow of the first frame is not shifted at all.
second: the shadow of the last frame is exactly centered below the marble.
